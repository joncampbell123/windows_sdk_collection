This test makes sure both reading and changeline
work on DMF floppies.

The test will do a check on one or two floppies.
NOTE: DMF Floppy1 must be in drive before starting,
and DMF Floppy2 must be interted into drive when
queried for.

Parameters

Drive Letter: the floppy drive to test

Number of Floppies: 1 or 2
