REDIAL.BAT performs a continuous redial test, pausing
for 30 seconds between redial attempts.  This test
must be run for a period of at least 72 hours to  ensure 
that your driver's connect/listen/hangup functionality is
stable.

usage

	redial ras_server_entry_in_phonebook log_file

The test assumes that the Administrator account has dial in
permission on the ras server.  If you have another account
for dialing in, edit redial.bat and use the appropriate
user account.
