Empty Folders Are Not Added to the Favorites Menu

 Last reviewed: October 30, 1997
Article ID: Q159710
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Internet Explorer Administration Kit, versions 3.2a and 4.0

SYMPTOMS

Empty folders are not added to the favorites menu, even though they are added in the Internet Explorer Administration Kit (IEAK) Wizard.

WORKAROUND

There is no workaround for this issue. The IEAK 3.0 Wizard does not support adding empty folders to the favorites menu.
