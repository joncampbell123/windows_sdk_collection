Changes Ignored When Editing a Recurring Appointment

 Last reviewed: June 17, 1997
Article ID: Q170221
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows CE version 1.0

SYMPTOMS

The changes you make while editing a recurring appointment may not be saved. Any changes made to the Ends time may be ignored.

RESOLUTION

To work around this problem, make all necessary changes to the Ends time before you click the Recur tab. If you click the Recur tab first, changes to the Ends time are ignored.

STATUS

Microsoft is researching this problem and will post new information here in the Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.
