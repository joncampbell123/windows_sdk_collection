Table Format Lost When Pasted into Pocket Word Document

 Last reviewed: April 14, 1997
Article ID: Q162378
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Pocket Word version 1.0

SYMPTOMS

When you copy a group of cells from Microsoft Pocket Excel to Pocket Word, the table format of the group of cells is not preserved.

CAUSE

Windows CE cannot copy a group of cells as a table. Instead, the contents of the group of cells is copied as tab-delimited text. In addition, Pocket Word does not support tables.

STATUS

Microsoft is researching this problem and will post new information here in the Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.
