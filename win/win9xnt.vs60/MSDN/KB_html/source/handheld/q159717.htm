Session Icons in Terminal May Be Partially Covered

 Last reviewed: April 14, 1997
Article ID: Q159717
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows CE version 1.0

SYMPTOMS

Session icons in Terminal may disappear or may be only partially visible.

CAUSE

This behavior occurs when the following three conditions are met:

  • You select Large Icon view.
  • A session's name consists of three or more lines of text.
  • There are enough session icons to cause them to wrap to another line on the screen.

RESOLUTION

Use one of the following methods to view the icons:

  • Use the ARROW keys to scroll through the icons.
  • Tap an icon or its name to display the entire icon.
  • Switch to Small Icon view or Details view in order to see all the icons.
