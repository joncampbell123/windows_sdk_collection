General Protection Fault After Installing Stacker 4.1

 Last reviewed: November 26, 1997
Article ID: Q176720
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Internet Explorer versions 4.0, 4.01 for Windows 95

SYMPTOMS

When you restart your computer after you install Stacker version 4.1, you may receive a general protection (GP) fault error message.

CAUSE

You may receive this error message if the host drive for your computer is too small.

RESOLUTION

To resolve this issue, increase the size of your host drive. For information about increasing the size of your host drive, see the documentation included with Stacker version 4.1, or contact Stac Electronics.
