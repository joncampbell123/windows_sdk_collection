How to Change Your Default E-mail Program in Internet Explorer

 Last reviewed: February 11, 1998
Article ID: Q180910
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Internet Explorer version 4.0 for Macintosh

SUMMARY

This article describes how to change your default e-mail program in Internet Explorer.

MORE INFORMATION

To change your default e-mail program, follow these steps:

  1. Start Internet Explorer, and then click Preferences on the Edit menu.

  2. Click Protocol Helpers.

  3. Click Mailto, click Change, and then click Change Helper.

  4. Click the appropriate e-mail program, and then click Open.

  5. Click OK, and then click OK again.
