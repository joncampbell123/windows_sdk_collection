Font May Not Change Size in Internet Explorer on UNIX

 Last reviewed: February 23, 1998
Article ID: Q181502
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Internet Explorer version 4.01 for UNIX on Sun Solaris

SYMPTOMS

If you click Fonts on the View menu and then click another font size, the font size on the page you are viewing may not change.

STATUS

Microsoft is researching this problem and will post new information here in the Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.
