Computer Stops Responding When Exporting Messages

 Last reviewed: October 1, 1997
Article ID: Q169988
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Outlook Express version 4.0 for Windows 95
  • Microsoft Outlook Express version 4.0 for Windows NT 4.0

SYMPTOMS

When you attempt to export messages from Outlook Express into Microsoft Exchange, your computer may stop responding (hang).

CAUSE

You may not have enough free space on your hard disk to complete this process.

RESOLUTION

Increase the amount of free space available on your hard disk, and then export the messages again.

STATUS

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Outlook Express version 4.0. We are researching this problem and will post new information here in the Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.
