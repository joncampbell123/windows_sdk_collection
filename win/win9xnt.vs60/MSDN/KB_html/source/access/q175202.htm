ACC97: Print Relationship Wizard Available on MSL

 Last reviewed: October 21, 1997
Article ID: Q175202
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Access 97

SUMMARY

The Print Relationship Wizard prints the contents of the Relationships window so that you can document the relationships between tables in your database. 

 ~ Prels80.exe (size: 580752 bytes) 

   is available for download from the Microsoft Software Library.
