ACC97: F5 Does Not Switch a Report from Design to Print Preview

 Last reviewed: May 30, 1997
Article ID: Q163061
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Access 97

SYMPTOMS

Novice: Requires knowledge of the user interface on single-user computers.

When you press the F5 key in Design view of a report, the report does not switch to Print Preview.

RESOLUTION

To switch a report from Design view to Print Preview, on the View menu, click Print Preview.

MORE INFORMATION

When you are in Design view of a form, you can press the F5 key to switch the form to Form view. However, the F5 key does not have this functionality in Design view of a report.

REFERENCES

For more information about using shortcut keys in Microsoft Access, search on the phrase "shortcut keys," and then "Use shortcut keys in Microsoft Access," or ask the Microsoft Office Assistant.
