SBFM97: List of Supported Accounting Programs

 Last reviewed: February 12, 1998
Article ID: Q164995
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Excel 97 for Windows
  • Microsoft Small Business Financial Manager 97 for Microsoft Excel 97

SUMMARY

Version 97 of the Microsoft Small Business Financial Manager for Microsoft Excel (SBFM) supports a wide variety of accounting programs from which it can import data. This article lists by operating system and version the accounting programs that the SBFM supports.
