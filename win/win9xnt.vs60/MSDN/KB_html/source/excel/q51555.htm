Excel: SHIFT+TAB Does Not Select Previous Field in Data Form

 Last reviewed: November 2, 1994
Article ID: Q51555

SUMMARY

Page 159 of the "Microsoft Excel Reference" version 2.2 manual and page 16 of the "Microsoft Excel Quick Reference Guide" version 2.2 manual incorrectly state that SHIFT+TAB moves to the previous field in a record when you are in a data form. SHIFT+TAB works as a normal tab in Excel data forms.

This feature is under review and will be considered for inclusion in a future release.
