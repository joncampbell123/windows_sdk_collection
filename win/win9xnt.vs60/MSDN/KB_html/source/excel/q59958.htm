XL2: Toggle Between Values and Formulas Incorrect in Manual

 Last reviewed: June 2, 1995
Article ID: Q59958
Page 426 of the "Microsoft Excel Reference" version 2.2 manual incorrectly states that you can toggle the display between values and formulas on a spreadsheet or macrosheet by pressing COMMAND+' (left single quotation mark). This key sequence will copy the formula from the cell directly above it. The correct key sequence is COMMAND+` (grave accent mark).
