XL7: AutoCalculate Not Available on Macro Sheet

 Last reviewed: September 13, 1996
Article ID: Q133415
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Excel for Windows 95, version 7.0

SUMMARY

The AutoCalculate feature is not available in a Microsoft Excel 4.0 macro sheet. This behavior is by design.

MORE INFORMATION

With Autocalculate, you do not need to use a calculator or enter temporary formulas on a worksheet when you want to quickly check a total. Now you can just select the range you want to sum, and the answer will appear in the status bar at the bottom of the screen. You can also average the selected numbers or count the entries by clicking the AutoCalculate area in the status bar with the right mouse button.
