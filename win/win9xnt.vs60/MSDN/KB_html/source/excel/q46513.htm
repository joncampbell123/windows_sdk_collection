Excel: Cannot Print from Data Form

 Last reviewed: November 29, 1994
Article ID: Q46513
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Excel for the Macintosh, versions 2.2, 3.0, 4.0

SUMMARY

You cannot print from a data form in Microsoft Excel version 2.20, 3.0 or 4.0.

However, you can format a section of the worksheet to look the same as the data form and extract the information to that area to be printed. You can do this with a command macro.

Alternatively, if a screen-dump desk utility is available, you can use it to obtain a printed copy of a data form as a graphic.

This feature is under review and will be considered for inclusion in a future release.
