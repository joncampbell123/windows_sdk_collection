How Q+E Displays Dates

 Last reviewed: October 31, 1994
Article ID: Q46017

SUMMARY

Q+E supports dates ranging from January 1, 1900, through December 31, 2099. When Q+E displays dates, it does so only in MM/DD/YY format. This cannot be changed. To differentiate between years in the 20th and 21st centuries, Q+E uses a capital letter in the tens place to denote that the year is in the 21st century.

The following is a list of dates showing how they display in Q+E: 

   Date            Q+E
   ----            ---

     1/1/1900      01/01/00
     2/1/1950      02/01/50
   12/25/1999      12/25/99
     1/1/2000      01/01/A0
   10/10/2010      10/10/B0
    3/17/2025      03/17/C5
   12/31/2099      12/31/J9
