Excel: Mislabeled Disks with Excel Courseware Developer's Kit

 Last reviewed: November 30, 1994
Article ID: Q88463
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Excel for Windows, version 4.0
  • Microsoft Excel for the Macintosh, version 4.0

SUMMARY

The Microsoft Excel Courseware Developer's Kit (CDK) may contain incorrectly labeled disks.

The disks labeled, "Word Step-by-Step Courseware Developer's Kit" should read, "Excel Step-by-Step Courseware Developer's Kit."

The information on these disks is correct, only the label is in error.

MORE INFORMATION

Designed to lower the cost of training for corporate accounts, the Excel CDK contains "Microsoft Excel 4.0 Step by Step" on disk, along with a license to customize and reproduce as many books as necessary. The CDK also includes classroom materials and an instructor's version of "Microsoft Excel 4.0 Step by Step." The Excel CDK is available through Microsoft Press.
