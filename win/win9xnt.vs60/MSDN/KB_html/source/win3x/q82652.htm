Da Vinci eMAIL GPF in DSWIN.DLL

 Last reviewed: November 21, 1994
Article ID: Q82652
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows operating system versions 3.0, 3.0a, 3.1, 3.11

SUMMARY

Da Vinci eMAIL may cause a general protection (GP) fault in DSWIN.DLL at 0009:0016 when you attach a file to a message and try to send the message to multiple aliases. Under Windows 3.0, Da Vinci eMAIL will cause an Unrecoverable Application Error (UAE).
