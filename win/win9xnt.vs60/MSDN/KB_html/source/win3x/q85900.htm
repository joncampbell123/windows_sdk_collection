Attributes Step is Incorrect in Windows/MS-DOS 5.0 Guide

 Last reviewed: November 23, 1994
Article ID: Q85900
The information in this article applies to: 

  - Microsoft Windows & MS-DOS 5 for PS/2

SUMMARY

Page 4 of the "Getting Started Guide for Microsoft Windows 3.1 & MS-DOS 5.0 for the IBM PS/2" states in step 12: 

   You must change the attributes of the five files listed in step 9
The correct statement should be: 

   You must change the attributes of the five files listed in step 10
Step 9 refers to OS/2 directories that may be present. Step 10 lists the five files that are referenced.
