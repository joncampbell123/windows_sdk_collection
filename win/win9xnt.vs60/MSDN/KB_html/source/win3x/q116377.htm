WFWG 3.11: Fax Machines That Are Incompatible with AWFAX

 Last reviewed: November 21, 1994
Article ID: Q116377
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows for Workgroups version 3.11

SUMMARY

The following fax machines are incompatible with Microsoft At Work PC Fax:

  • Hitachi HiFax 17
  • Omnifax 9s Series
  • Omnifax G90 Series

MORE INFORMATION

Hitachi HiFax 17

The Hitachi HiFax 17 fails during the negotiation stage and generates an "Undeliverable Mail" error message.

Omnifax 9s Series and G90 Series

Both of the above Omnifax machines fail during the negotiation stage when receiving faxes from Microsoft At Work PC Fax.
