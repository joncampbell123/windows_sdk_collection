TopDesk Window May Not Retain Its Size or Position

 Last reviewed: November 22, 1994
Article ID: Q86876
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows operating system versions 3.1, 3.11

SYMPTOMS

The TopDesk utility, which is included with the Windows Resource Kit (WRK) for Windows 3.1, will not retain resized or repositioned window information unless you close TopDesk before you exit Windows.

WORKAROUND

To work around this problem:

  1. Resize and/or reposition TopDesk as desired.

  2. From the Options menu, choose Preferences.

  3. Choose the OK button.

Your changes will be saved, and you won't have to quit and restart TopDesk for the window information to be saved.
