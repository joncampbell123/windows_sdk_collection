WFWG Hangs at Startup with Future Domain 32-Bit Disk Access

 Last reviewed: November 21, 1994
Article ID: Q106366
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows for Workgroups version 3.11

SYMPTOMS

Versions earlier than 1.02 of Future Domain's 32-bit disk access drivers may cause Windows for Workgroups to stop responding (hang) at startup.

RESOLUTION

Disable Future Domain's 32-bit disk drivers until you obtain an updated driver(s).

MORE INFORMATION

For more information on obtaining updated 32-bit disk access drivers for Future Domain controllers, contact Future Domain at one of the following numbers:
