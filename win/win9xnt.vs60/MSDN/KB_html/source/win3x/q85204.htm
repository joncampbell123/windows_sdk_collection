 Last reviewed: November 21, 1994
Article ID: Q85204
The information in this article applies to:

SUMMARY

This article contains the license agreement for the video and printer drivers available in the Microsoft Windows operating system version 3.1 Windows Drivers Library (WDL).

For a complete listing of the WDL contents and instructions for obtaining the WDL, query on the following word in the Microsoft Knowledge Base: 

   getwdl
