Internet Control Pack Is Not Supported by Microsoft

 Last reviewed: January 11, 1997
Article ID: Q156422
The information in this article applies to:
  • Standard, Professional, and Enterprise Editions of Microsoft Visual Basic, 16-bit and 32-bit, for Windows, version 4.0

SUMMARY

The Internet Control Pack is a beta product that is not supported by Microsoft. The product was developed by NetManage Inc. and licensed to Microsoft. Contact NetManage for support on the Internet Control Pack.
