Custom BallPoint Clips for Laptops

 Last reviewed: September 16, 1996
Article ID: Q88703
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft BallPoint Mouse, version 1.0

SUMMARY

Custom designed clips for the Microsoft BallPoint Mouse are available for the machines listed below. The new clips are one piece rather than two, are smaller than the original clips, and enable you to close the computer's lid with the clips and Ballpoint Mouse in place. To obtain a custom clip for your computer, call Microsoft Sales Information Center (MSIC) at (800) 426-9400.
