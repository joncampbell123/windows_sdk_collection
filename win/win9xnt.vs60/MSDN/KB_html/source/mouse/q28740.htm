Obtaining Hosiden Connectors Used with the Bus Mouse

 Last reviewed: September 16, 1996
Article ID: Q28740
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Bus Mouse, version 1.0
  • Microsoft Mouse Driver for MS-DOS, versions 6.x, 7.x, and 8.0

SUMMARY

This information applies to the 200 series and 400 series Microsoft Bus Mouse for MS-DOS systems.

The Hosiden connectors and sockets, which are used with the Bus Mouse with InPort hardware, can be obtained from HB Associates.

HB Associates sell the Hosiden connectors and sockets separately
