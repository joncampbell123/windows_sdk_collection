Ventura Publisher 2.0 and Mouse Driver 6.24b

 Last reviewed: September 16, 1996
Article ID: Q63224
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Mouse Driver for MS-DOS, version 6.24b

SUMMARY

When mouse driver version 6.24b for the Microsoft Mouse is installed, Ventura Publisher version 2.0 is limited to black and white regardless of the screen colors selected.

To correct this behavior, replace mouse driver 6.24b with mouse driver 7.04 or later.
