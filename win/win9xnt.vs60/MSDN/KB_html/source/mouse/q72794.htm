50 Mouse Functions Available, Not 53 (Documentation Error)

 Last reviewed: September 16, 1996
Article ID: Q72794
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Mouse Driver for MS-DOS, versions 8.x

SUMMARY

On page 18 of the second edition of the "Microsoft Mouse Programmer's Reference," the manual incorrectly states that there are 53 mouse functions available for programming in version 8.x of the Microsoft Mouse driver for MS-DOS systems.

There are actually 50 mouse functions available, with functions 17, 18, and 46 unavailable.
