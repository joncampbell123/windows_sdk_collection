SUMMARY

This article lists the .CAB filenames and sizes and the files that the .CAB files contain for Microsoft Office version 4.2c for Windows disks 11-20 (20 disks total) for the 3.5-inch DMF disks.

(Note that Disk 1 and Disk 20 are standard 1.44-MB formatted disks; disks 2 through 19 are DMF formatted.)
