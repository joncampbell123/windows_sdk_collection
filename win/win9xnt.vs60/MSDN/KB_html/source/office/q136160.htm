OFF: Title of Floating Office Shortcut Bar Changes

 Last reviewed: June 24, 1997
Article ID: Q136160
7.00 97 WINDOWS kbtool

The information in this article applies to:

  • Microsoft Office for Windows 95, version 7.0
  • Microsoft Office 97 for Windows

SYMPTOMS

The title for the Office Shortcut Bar may vary according to the size of the Office Shortcut Bar window.

MORE INFORMATION

When the Office Shortcut Bar is floating (that is, when it's not anchored to the side of your screen) it may have any one of the following titles:

  • Microsoft Office Shortcut Bar
  • Office Shortcut Bar
  • Shortcut Bar
  • Office
  • MS

NOTE: When the Office Shortcut Bar is docked, it does not have a title.
