OFF97: ErrMsg: MSCam Cannot Use the Audio Settings Specified

 Last reviewed: March 18, 1998
Article ID: Q182555
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Office 97 for Windows

SYMPTOMS

When you attempt to record an Audio-Video Interleaved (AVI) file in Microsoft Camcorder, you may receive the following messages: 

   MSCam cannot use the audio settings specified

    -and-

   Capture failed; Please check for disk space and try again

CAUSE

This problem may occur when there is a conflict with other video capture devices or when the video capture device is damaged.

WORKAROUND

To work around this problem, follow these steps:

  1. Uninstall Microsoft Camcorder by using Add/Remove Programs icon in Control Panel. To do this, follow these steps: 

          a. On the Start menu, point to Settings, and then click Control

             Panel.

      b. Double-click Add/Remove Programs.

      c. Click Microsoft Camcorder and click Add/Remove.

         This step starts the Setup program.

      d. In the Microsoft Camcorder Setup dialog box, click
         Remove All.


  2. On the Start menu, point to Settings, and then click Control Panel.


  3. Double-click Multimedia.


  4. Click the Advanced tab and click Video Capture Devices.


  5. Select Screen Capture Device Driver for AVI and click Properties. Click
   Remove.


  6. Disable any other listed Video Capture devices.


  7. Reinstall Microsoft Camcorder from the ValuPack folder on the
   Microsoft PowerPoint or Microsoft Office compact disc.

    

STATUS

 


Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in the Microsoft products
listed at the beginning of this article.
