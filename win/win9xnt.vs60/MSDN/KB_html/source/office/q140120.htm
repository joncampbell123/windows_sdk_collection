Upgrade to Office Pro From Office Standard and Access 7.0

 Last reviewed: March 20, 1997
Article ID: Q140120
7.00 WINDOWS kbpolicy kbsetup

The information in this article applies to:

  • Microsoft Office Professional for Windows 95, version 7.0
  • Microsoft Office Standard for Windows 95, version 7.0
  • Microsoft Access version 7.0

SUMMARY

Customers who purchase Microsoft Office Standard for Windows 95 and Microsoft Access 7.0 separately can purchase the Microsoft Office Professional for Windows 95 disk set by calling the Microsoft Sales and Information Center (MSIC) at (800) 426-9400.

MORE INFORMATION

The replacement cost of the Office Professional disk set is (U.S.) $15.00 and $5.00 shipping and handling plus sales tax. The cost of the compact disc is (U.S.) $26.95 or (CDN) $39.95 and $5.00 shipping and handling.
