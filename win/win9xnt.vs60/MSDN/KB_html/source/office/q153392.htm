Contents of The Microsoft Empowerment Pack for the Mac

 Last reviewed: November 17, 1997
Article ID: Q153392
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Office for the Macintosh, versions 4.2, 4.2.1, 4.2.1a, 4.2.1b

SUMMARY

This article lists the contents of the compact disc that ships with The Microsoft Empowerment Pack for the Macintosh version 1.0.

MORE INFORMATION

The Microsoft Empowerment Pack for the Macintosh is being shipped to all registered users of Microsoft Office for the Macintosh. The Empowerment Pack includes a single compact disc that contains tools for connecting to the Internet, for improving performance of the Office programs, and for upgrading your system software.

The following is an overview of the items on the root level of the Empowerment Pack CD-ROM disc:
