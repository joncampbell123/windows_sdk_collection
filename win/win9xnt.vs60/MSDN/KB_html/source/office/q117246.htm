Database Access User's Guide Not Included on Office 3.0-CD

 Last reviewed: November 17, 1997
Article ID: Q117246
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Office for Macintosh, version 3.0

SUMMARY

Microsoft Office 3.0 for Macintosh on CD lists that the following three manuals are available for Microsoft Excel on the CD:

  • "Microsoft Excel User's Guide"
  • "Microsoft Excel Function Reference"
  • "Database Access User's Guide"

However, the disk includes only two manuals "Microsoft Excel User's Guide" and "Microsoft Excel Function Reference."

MORE INFORMATION

Page 39 of the online "Microsoft Excel User's Guide," version 4.0-CD, states that three manuals are available on the CD. However, the "Database Access User's Guide" was not included on the CD.

To obtain this manual, call the Microsoft Order Desk at (800) 360-7561 and request the "Database Access User's Guide." If you are outside the United States, contact the Microsoft subsidiary for your areaTo locate your subsidiary, see the Microsoft World Wide Offices Web site at: 

   http://www.microsoft.com/worldwide/default.htm

REFERENCES

"Microsoft Excel User's Guide," version 4.0-CD, page 39
