Small Business Pack Help Only Available Through Shortcut Bar

 Last reviewed: March 20, 1997
Article ID: Q141779
7.00 WINDOWS kbtool kbusage

The information in this article applies to:

  • Microsoft Office Small Business Pack for Windows 95, version 1.0
  • Microsoft Office for Windows 95, version 7.0

SYMPTOMS

When working with the templates that are included with the Microsoft Office Small Business Pack, Small Business Pack Help will only be available through the Office Small Business Pack toolbar on the Microsoft Office Shortcut Bar.

CAUSE

When you open a template from the Small Business Pack, you will access the Help for the individual application that the template is for; you will not access Microsoft Office Small Business Pack Help.

RESOLUTION

To access either the Answer Wizard or Help for the Small Business Pack, the Small Business Pack toolbar must be activated on the Microsoft Office Shortcut Bar. After activating or selecting the toolbar, there will be an icon/shortcut for help that appears to be a book with a question mark on it. Selecting this icon will bring help for the Small Business Pack available to the user.
