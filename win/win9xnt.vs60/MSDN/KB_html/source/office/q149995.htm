List of Templates Included with Office Small Business Pack

 Last reviewed: September 16, 1996
Article ID: Q149995
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Office Small Business Pack for Office 95, version 1.0
  • Microsoft Office Professional for Windows 95, version 7.0

SUMMARY

The Microsoft Office Small Business Pack for Office 95 is an add-in product for Microsoft Office Professional for Windows 95. The Office Small Business Pack provides task-based tools such as business templates, forms, and reports to help you plan, manage, and analyze your business.

You can access the Office Small Business Pack templates from the Office Shortcut Bar and from within Microsoft Office Professional programs. Because the Office Small Business Pack is integrated with Office Help, you can also access the Small Business Pack templates through the Answer Wizard.

MORE INFORMATION

The following table lists all of the templates, documents, presentations, and databases that are installed by the Office Small Business Pack Setup program.
