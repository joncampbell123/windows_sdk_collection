OFF97: Files Installed and Updated in MS Office 97 SR-1 Patch

 Last reviewed: March 13, 1998
Article ID: Q172387
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Office 97 for Windows, Service Release 1 (SR-1)

SUMMARY

This article contains a list of the files that are updated or installed when you install the Microsoft Office 97 SR-1 Patch.

MORE INFORMATION
