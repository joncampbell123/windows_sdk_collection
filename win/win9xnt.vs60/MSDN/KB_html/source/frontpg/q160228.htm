FP: Bullet Points, Numbers Hidden When Font Size Increased

 Last reviewed: March 18, 1998
Article ID: Q160228
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft FrontPage 97 for Windows with Bonus Pack

SYMPTOMS

When you select a bulleted or numbered list by double-clicking in the area to the left of the list, it is possible to increase the font size of the list items to the point that the bullet points or numbers in the list are hidden.

WORKAROUND

To see the bullet points or numbers, click Refresh on the View menu in FrontPage Editor.

STATUS

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in the Microsoft products listed at the beginning of this article. This problem was corrected in Microsoft FrontPage 98 for Windows.
