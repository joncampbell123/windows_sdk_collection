FP1: How to Delete Custom Template in Macintosh FrontPage

 Last reviewed: March 17, 1998
Article ID: Q174941
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft FrontPage for the Power Macintosh, version 1.0

SUMMARY

This article describes how to remove custom templates from FrontPage.

NTOE: If you delete or alter built-in templates, you will need to reinstall FrontPage to restore the templates.

MORE INFORMATION

To remove a custom template, follow these steps:

  1. Open the Macintosh HD:Microsoft FrontPage:Pages folder.

  2. Find the template you want to delete. For example, if you want to delete a custom template called Sales, find the Sales.tem folder.

  3. Drag the folder to the Trash icon.
