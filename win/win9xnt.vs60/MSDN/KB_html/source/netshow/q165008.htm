No Audio Data Has Been Sent from... Err Msg w/Real Time Encoder

 Last reviewed: September 16, 1997
Article ID: Q165008
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft NetShow version 2.0

SYMPTOMS

When you use the Real Time Encoder, you may get the following error message: 

   No audio data has been sent from the input device for multiple 
   seconds.

CAUSE

The audio input is not properly selected in the Recording mixer device or the encoding settings are overloading the CPU.

WORKAROUND

If the audio input is not properly selected, check the Recording mixer to view the inputs that are selected.

If the encoding settings are overloading the CPU, use the settings under the Advanced button on the Wizard page for selecting the video codec. You may need to reduce your window size, decrease the picture quality, choose a optimal pixel format for your capture device, or reduce the frames per second and or increase the IFrame/Second value.

NOTE: One suggestion or a combination of suggestions may need to be changed for optimal results.
