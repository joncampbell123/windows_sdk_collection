XFOR: Can't Mail to Exchange Account "Admin" from MSMail Client

 Last reviewed: November 18, 1997
Article ID: Q164464
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Exchange Server, versions 4.0 and 5.0

SYMPTOMS

Mail messages sent to a Microsoft Exchange Server account named Admin from an MSMail client through the MSMail Connector do not arrive in the Admin's mailbox on Exchange Server.

CAUSE

This is by design. Mail destined for the Exchange Admin account is sent to the alias listed in the Administrator entry on the MSMail Connector Interchange.
