XADM: X.400 Connector Enabled on Standard Version of Exchange

 Last reviewed: November 14, 1997
Article ID: Q174756
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Exchange Server, version 4.0, 5.0, and 5.5

SUMMARY

When a mix of Standard and Enterprise versions of Microsoft Exchange Server are installed in the same Microsoft Exchange Site, the X.400 Connector is enabled for all Servers in the Site. This happens regardless of the order in which Standard Edition and Enterprise Edition Exchange Servers are installed. This is by product design.
