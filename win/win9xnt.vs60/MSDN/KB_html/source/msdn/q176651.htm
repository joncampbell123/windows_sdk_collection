INFO: Connecting to the MSDN Online

 Last reviewed: November 12, 1997
Article ID: Q176651
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Developer Network Subscriptions

SUMMARY

MSDN Online is the start page for developers, with headline news, product information, and resources. The MSDN Online Membership allows you to access valuable additional content from MSDN, such as:

  • MSDN Library Online
  • ISV Software Downloads
  • Microsoft Mastering Series Training
  • Great Discounts
  • MSDN Flash
  • Tip of the Week

To gain access to these resources, you need to fill out registration information at the following URL: 

   http://premium.microsoft.com/register/msdn/register.asp
If you are having registration problems or you have additional questions about MSDN Online, see the following URL: 

   http://www.microsoft.com/msdn/services/faq.htm
For more information about the membership benefits of MSDN Online, go to the following URL: 

   http://www.microsoft.com/msdn/services/benefits.htm
