WD98: Office Assistant Animations Start Slowly

 Last reviewed: February 17, 1998
Article ID: Q177929
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word 98 Macintosh Edition

SYMPTOMS

When you activate the Microsoft Office Assistant, the Assistant will activate slowly and any sound effects may not be synchronized with the image when it is displayed.

Although this problem occurs with all of the Office Assistants, it most obvious with the following Office Assistants: 

   Bosgrove
   F1
   Earl
   PowerPup
   Rocky

CAUSE

There is a slow interface between Microsoft Office Assistant and the Macintosh operating system.

STATUS

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in the Microsoft products listed at the beginning of this article. We are researching this problem and will post new information here in the Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.

MORE INFORMATION

For more information about the Office Assistant, click the Office Assistant, type "Office Assistant," click Search, and then click to view the "Get Help, tips, and messages through the Office Assistant" topic.

NOTE: If the Assistant is hidden, click the Office Assistant button on the Standard toolbar. If Word Help is not installed on your computer, please see the following article in the Microsoft Knowledge Base: 

   ARTICLE-ID: Q120802
   TITLE     : Office: How to Add/Remove a Single Office
               Program or Component
