Scroll Bar and Status Bar Not Update with WordPerfect Help

 Last reviewed: February 5, 1998
Article ID: Q110072
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word for Windows, version 6.0

SYMPTOMS

In Word 6.0 for Windows, the scroll bar will not move and the status bar will not update when Help for WordPerfect Users is turned on. (To locate this option, choose Options from the Tools menu and select the General tab.)

RESOLUTION

Turn off WordPerfect Help. Pressing a letter or space will cause the display to update and bring the insertion point into view.

STATUS

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Word version 6.0. This problem was corrected in Word for Windows version 6.0a.
