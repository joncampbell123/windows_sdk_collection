WD6X: DOCERR: Customize Tip of the Day

 Last reviewed: February 5, 1998
Article ID: Q140176
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word for Windows, versions 6.0, 6.0a, 6.0c
  • Microsoft Word for the Macintosh, versions 6.0, 6.0.1, 6.0.1a
  • Microsoft Word for Windows NT, version 6.0

SUMMARY

The "Microsoft Word 6 Resource Kit" (page 528) and the "Microsoft Word 6 Resource Kit," second edition, (page 727) incorrectly state the following: 

   All of the tips are accessible via Word's Help engine, and you can
   even customize the list to include your own tips.
You cannot customize the tips. The tips are hardcoded into the Wwintl.dll file.

REFERENCES

"Microsoft Word 6.0 for Windows Resource Kit," p. 528 "Microsoft Word 6 Resource Kit," second edition, p. 727
