Medical/Legal Dictionaries Available That Contain All MS Words

 Last reviewed: October 10, 1997
Article ID: Q62195
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word for the Macintosh, versions 4.0, 5.0, 5.1

SUMMARY

The available Microsoft Word for the Macintosh medical and legal dictionaries for versions 4.0, 5.0 and 5.1 contain all the words included in the MS Dictionary in addition to their own special terms. (Microsoft foreign dictionaries do not contain the regular MS Dictionary words.)

These supplemental dictionaries allow you to check the spelling of any special terms in a document without having to switch between two or more dictionaries to accurately spell check your entire document.

Note: The Microsoft Word version 4.0 medical and legal dictionaries are not compatible with Microsoft Word version 5.0 or 5.1.

To order one of these dictionaries, contact Alki Software at (800) 669-9673 or (206) 286-2600.

For technical support of those dictionaries, please call Alki Software Support, Monday through Friday, 9 A.M. to 5 P.M. Pacific Standard Time, at (206) 286-2780.
