SGML: Files Installed During Setup

 Last reviewed: August 5, 1997
Article ID: Q126082
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft SGML Author for Word, version 1.0
  • Microsoft Word for Windows, versions 6.0, 6.0a, 6.0c

SUMMARY

This article lists the files that are installed when you setup SGML Author, either when you choose the Complete option or the Converter Only option.
