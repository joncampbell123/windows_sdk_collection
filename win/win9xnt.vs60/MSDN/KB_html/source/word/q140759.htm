Word 7.0 for Windows 95: Contents of Disks 1-9

 Last reviewed: August 4, 1997
Article ID: Q140759
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word for Windows 95, Version 7.0

SUMMARY

This article lists the .cab file names and sizes and the files that the .cab files contain for Word for Windows 95, version 7.0. This article includes information about Disks 1-9 of the 3.5-inch DMF disks.

Disk 1 is a standard 1.44 MB formatted disk; disks 2 through 9 are DMF formatted.

For more information about .cab files, please see the following articles in the Microsoft Knowledge Base: 

   ARTICLE ID:    Q120006
   TITLE     :    How to Copy Files from Cabinets on DMF-Formatted Disks
