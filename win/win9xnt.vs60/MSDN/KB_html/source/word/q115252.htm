No CAPS LOCK or NUM LOCK Indicator in Word 6.0

 Last reviewed: February 5, 1998
Article ID: Q115252
6.00 6.00a WINDOWS kbusage

The information in this article applies to:

  • Microsoft Word for Windows, versions 6.0, 6.0a

In Word 2.x for Windows, the status bar indicates when CAPS LOCK or NUM LOCK are enabled on the computer keyboard. In Word 6.0 for Windows there is no indicator on the status bar when CAPS LOCK or NUM LOCK are on.

This feature is under review and will be considered for inclusion in a future release.
