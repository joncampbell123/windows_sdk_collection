WD6x: Word 6.0.1 Components for Typical, Complete, Minimum Setup

 Last reviewed: February 6, 1998
Article ID: Q129344
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word for the Macintosh, version 6.0.1

SUMMARY

This article summarizes the Word components that Setup installs for each option: Typical, Complete, and Minimum.

This information, including filenames, also appears in the Read Me file. To open the Read Me file, either choose its icon in the folder that Microsoft Word is installed in or open the document from within Microsoft Word. The Read Me file is a Microsoft Word document.
