OLE DLL Files Installed by Word 6.0 for Windows

 Last reviewed: February 6, 1998
Article ID: Q141875
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word for Windows, versions 6.0, 6.0a, 6.0c

SUMMARY

This article lists the OLE files installed by Word 6.x for Windows.

When you troubleshoot certain types of problems, it may be necessary to rename or delete these files. To reinstall these files, reinstall Word.

Note: Before you rename or delete these files, be sure to quit all Office applications, including the Microsoft Office Shortcut Bar.

MORE INFORMATION

Files used only by Word: 

   Sdm.dll
   Ctl3dv2.dll
   Shareres.dll
   Ttembed.dll
OLE 2.0 files used by other applications, in addition to Word: 

   Compobj.dll
   Ole2.dll
   Ole2.reg
   Ole2conv.dll
   Ole2disp.dll
   Ole2nls.dll
   Ole2prox.dll
   Storage.dll
