System: Common Printers and Their Printable Areas

 Last reviewed: February 2, 1998
Article ID: Q80799
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word for the Macintosh, versions 3.0, 3.01, 3.02, 4.0, 5.0, 5.1
  • Microsoft Word for the Macintosh, versions 6.0, 6.0.1, 6.0.1a
  • Microsoft Word 98 Macintosh Edition

SUMMARY

The tables in the "More Information" section of this article list printers commonly used with the Macintosh and their printable regions when printing from Microsoft Word for the Macintosh. In some cases these printable regions may vary from the regions specified by the printer manufacturer.

NOTE: With legal-sized paper, many printers will not print headers and footers in Word unless Larger Print Area is chosen in Page Setup. This is due to the standard Header/Footer settings in Word of .5 inches. Also, note that some color printers, such as the QMS ColorScript and the Hewlett- Packard (HP) PaintJet, have smaller printable areas and may not use the default Header/Footer setting in Word to print the header or footer.
