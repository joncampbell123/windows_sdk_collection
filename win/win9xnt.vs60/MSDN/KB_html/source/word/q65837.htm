Using the {print} Field with Dot-Matrix Printers and Winword

 Last reviewed: July 30, 1997
Article ID: Q65837
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word for Windows, versions 1.0, 1.1, 1.1a, 2.0, 2.0a, 2.0a-CD, 2.0b, 2.0c, 6.0, 6.0a, 6.0c
  • Microsoft Word for Windows 95, versions 7.0, 7.0a
  • Microsoft Word 97 for Windows

SUMMARY

You can use the {print} field with a dot-matrix printer only if the commands sent in the {print} field do not take the printer out of graphics mode.

Note: Most commands sent to a dot-matrix printer through the {print} field will take the printer out of graphics mode. For a list of commands that will not take the printer out of graphics mode, consult your printer owner's manual or contact the manufacturer.

For more information on the {print} field, refer to Page 104 of the "Microsoft Word for Windows User's Reference" guide.
