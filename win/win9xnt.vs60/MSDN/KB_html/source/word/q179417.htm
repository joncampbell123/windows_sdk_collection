WD97: Format Font Option in Find and Replace Does Not Work

 Last reviewed: January 19, 1998
Article ID: Q179417
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word 97 for Windows

SYMPTOMS

When you perform a find or replace operation (on the Edit menu) and you try to select Font in the Format drop-down list, nothing happens or Word stops responding.

NOTE: You may need to click More to see the Format drop-down list.

CAUSE

This problem may occur if you have TextBridge Pro 98 software installed.

WORKAROUND

To work around this problem, uninstall the TextBridge Pro 98 software.

For additional information about how to do this, please contact Xerox Corporation.
