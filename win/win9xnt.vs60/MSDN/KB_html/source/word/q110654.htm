GP Fault While Scanning with OmniPage Direct

 Last reviewed: February 5, 1998
Article ID: Q110654
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word for Windows, versions 6.0, 6.0a, 6.0c

SYMPTOMS

You receive a general protection (GP) fault in Word version 6.0 for Windows while scanning into a Word document using OmniPage or OmniPage 1.0, Direct AnyFont OCR for Logitech 1.0(DFL).

CAUSE

Word 6.0 is not registered with DFL.

RESOLUTION

To correct this problem:

  1. Remark out the following Word 2.0 for Windows lines (if found) in the SETTING.INI file. The SETTING.INI file is found in the \DIRECT (or \OMNIPAGE in version 1.0) subdirectory. 

          OpusApp=1,1,1,2229(for 2.0)
      OpusApp=1,1,1,2266(for 2.0b)
      OpusApp=1,1,1,2266

  2. Add the following line to the SETTING.INI file: 

          OpusApp=1,1,1,108

-or-

  1. Use the following procedure to register the new version of Word with DFL (instructions are also in the DFL manual):

    a. Run Direct and minimize.

    b. Run Word and minimize.

    c. Select the Direct icon by clicking once, and you will have an option 

          to register.

    d. Select Register, Word Processor, and Register again.

    e. Your cursor will turn into a dough-nut shape, click this on the 

          minimized Word 6.0 icon.

    f. Close Direct and restart.

As an alternative, you can contact CAERE Products to obtain an updated template with Word 6.0 macros.

For more information, contact Logitech technical support.
