Incorrect Bullets, Numbering After Changing Default Style

 Last reviewed: February 5, 1998
Article ID: Q137668
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word for Windows 95, version 7.0

SYMPTOMS

When you use AutoCorrect to apply bullets or numbers, and you then modify the font size and typeface of the Normal style, the bullets or numbers do not reflect this change.

STATUS

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Word for Windows 95, version 7.0. Microsoft is researching this problem and will post new information here in the Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.

WORKAROUND

Remove and then reapply the bullets or numbering format.
