SUMMARY

This article contains the directory of the 5.25-inch, high-density (1.2 MB) Disk #4 (disk 4 of 11) for version 6.0a of Word for Windows. The article includes the decompressed filename for each file, which you must provide when you use the DECOMP.EXE utility to manually decompress a file.

MORE INFORMATION
