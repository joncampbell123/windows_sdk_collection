WD: Wrong Left And Right Indents on Laser Labels 5660 in MacWord

 Last reviewed: February 3, 1998
Article ID: Q140182
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word for the Macintosh, versions 6.0, 6.0.1
  • Microsoft Word 98 Macintosh Edition

SYMPTOMS

When you create labels using the Avery Laser Label #5660, the labels are formatted with left and right indents of 0.43 inch instead of 0.26 inch.

WORKAROUND

Create a new document with the Avery Laser Label #5660, select the entire document, and then change the left and right indents to 0.26 inch.

STATUS

We are researching this problem and will post new information here in the Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.
