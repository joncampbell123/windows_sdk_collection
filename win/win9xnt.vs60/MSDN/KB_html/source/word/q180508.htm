WD98: Additional Word Templates Available in ValuPack Folder

 Last reviewed: February 17, 1998
Article ID: Q180508
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word 98 Macintosh Edition

SUMMARY

This article describes the additional templates that are available with the Microsoft Office 98 and Microsoft Word 98 compact discs in the ValuPack folder. These templates are located in the following folder on the Office 98 and Word 98 compact discs: 

   ValuPack:Templates:Word:Other Documents

   and

   ValuPack:Templates:Word:Windows

MORE INFORMATION

To use the templates, you can either copy them from the compact disc to the Microsoft Word 98:Templates folder or you can use the Value Pack installer to add the Template Component.

To use the Value Pack Installer, double-click the icon labeled Value Pack Installer.

The following are the templates located in the ValuPack:Templates:Word:Other Documents folder: 

   Agenda Wizard
   Brochure
   Calendar Wizard
   Invoice
   Invoice (AUS)
   Invoice (UK)
   Manual
   Press Release
   Purchase Order
   Purchase Order (AUS)
   Purchase Order (UK)
   Thesis
   Legal Pleading Wizard
   Newsletter Wizard
