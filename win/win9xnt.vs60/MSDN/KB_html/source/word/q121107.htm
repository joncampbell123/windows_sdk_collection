WD6x: Doc Error: Coupon for WDK Not Included in Retail Package

 Last reviewed: February 5, 1998
Article ID: Q121107
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word for the Macintosh, version 6.0

SUMMARY

Page 783 of the Microsoft Word "User's Guide" for version 6.0 for the Macintosh incorrectly states: 

   You can order the "Microsoft Word Developer's Kit" with a coupon
   included in your Word package.
The coupon is not included in the Word package.

MORE INFORMATION

The "Microsoft Word Developer's Kit" is a resource that is used for writing macros and other advanced Word tools.

The Word Developer's Kit is available wherever computer books are sold and directly from Microsoft Press. You can reach Microsoft Press at: (800) MS- PRESS or (615) 793-5090. Be sure to order/purchase the Second Edition of the "Microsoft Word Developer's Kit," which includes the Macintosh Word information.

REFERENCES

"Microsoft Word User's Guide," version 6.0, page 783
