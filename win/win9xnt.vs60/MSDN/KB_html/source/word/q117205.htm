WD6x: Incorrect Newsletter Wizard Information in Quick Results

 Last reviewed: February 5, 1998
Article ID: Q117205
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word for Windows, versions 6.0, 6.0a

There are two documentation errors on page 98 of the "Microsoft Word Quick Results" manual:

  • The first bulleted item states that the Newsletter Wizard offers three newsletter styles: "Classic, Modern, or Jazzy." The Newsletter Wizard actually offers only Classic and Modern.
  • The fourth bulleted item states "Specify if you plan to print or copy the newsletter on both sides of the paper." In fact, the wizard does not ask this question, so the item appears in error.
