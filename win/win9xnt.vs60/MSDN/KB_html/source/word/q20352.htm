Diablo Printers Supported and Features Available

 Last reviewed: July 30, 1997
Article ID: Q20352
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Word for the Macintosh, versions 1.0, 1.05, 3.0 , 3.01 3.02, 4.0

SUMMARY

Microsoft Word supports the Diablo 630, the Diablo 630 API, and the Diablo 630 ECS printers in versions 1.0, 1.05, 3.0, 3.01, and 3.02 through special printer drivers shipped with Word.

Both the Diablo 630 printer driver and the Typewriter driver can be used to drive these printers. With the Diablo driver, you can get superscript and subscript; with the Typewriter driver, you can get bold and underline. You cannot combine the features from both drivers.

Word version 4.0 supports these printers with a third-party printer driver called Mac Daisy Link; to get this driver, call Microsoft Sales Information Center at (800) 426-9400.
