SMC 8016 PC Card May Not Work with NEC Versa 6000H/IBM 760ED

 Last reviewed: November 8, 1996
Article ID: Q159035
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows 95 OEM Service Release versions 2, 2.1

SYMPTOMS

When you have an SMC 8016 Ethernet PC card installed in a NEC Versa 6000H or an IBM 760ed ThinkPad laptop computer in CardBus mode, the computer may stop responding (hang), or the card may not function correctly.

CAUSE

The SMC 8016 Ethernet PC card has a problem with driver initialization in CardBus mode, which may cause the drivers not to load.

RESOLUTION

Use another Ethernet PC card, or the newer SMC 8020 Ethernet PC card.

MORE INFORMATION

The combination of a TI 1130 CardBus controller and an SMC 8016 driver may have this problem.

Contact SMC, Inc. for additional information.
