U.S. Atlas Version 4.0 Does Not Play Video Clips in Windows 95

 Last reviewed: August 22, 1995
Article ID: Q132774
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows 95

SYMPTOMS

The video clips in U.S. Atlas version 4.0 do not play in Windows 95. The viewing area in U.S. Atlas is black and the video control buttons are unavailable.

CAUSE

U.S. Atlas version 4.0 is designed to run with Video For Windows version 1.0 only.

RESOLUTION

Contact the manufacturer for an upgrade. U.S. Atlas version 5.0 operates properly in Windows 95.
