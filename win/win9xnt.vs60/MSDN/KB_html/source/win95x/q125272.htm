Determining Whether NEC Monitors Are Energy Star Compliant

 Last reviewed: April 17, 1996
Article ID: Q125272
kb3rdparty kbdisplay

The information in this article applies to:

  • Microsoft Windows 95

Some NEC monitors are compliant with the Energy Star specifications. These monitors include the FGe and V series, with the exception of the 3FGe model. To determine if the monitor in question is Energy Star compliant, verify that the model number for the monitor ends in "-03". If the monitor model number does not end in "-03", the monitor is not compliant.
