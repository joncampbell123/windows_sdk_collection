BITFAT Blue Screen Error with Compressed Floppy Disk

 Last reviewed: August 31, 1995
Article ID: Q135946
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows 95
  • Microsoft Plus! for Windows 95

SYMPTOMS

When you use a floppy disk that was compressed with DriveSpace 3, you may receive the following error message: 

   Drvspace Error: Drive A: (F:\DRVSPACE.000). An error occurred while
   reading or writing the bitfat on this drive. To fix the problem, run
   ScanDisk and perform a thorough test of drive A and its host drive F.

CAUSE

This error message may occur because of a misalignment of the heads on the floppy drive or because of a problem with the floppy disk.

RESOLUTION

The error message may be safely ignored. If you continue to receive the error message, use one of the following resolutions:

  • Reformat the floppy disk.

    -or-

  • Have the floppy drive checked by your hardware manufacturer.
