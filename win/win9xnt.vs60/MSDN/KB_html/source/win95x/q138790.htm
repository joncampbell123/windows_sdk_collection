Envelopes Printed Incorrectly on HP DeskJet 540 Printer

 Last reviewed: September 19, 1997
Article ID: Q138790
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows 95

SYMPTOMS

When you print an envelope on a Hewlett-Packard DeskJet 540 printer, the output may not appear as you expect.

RESOLUTION

To resolve this problem, obtain an updated DeskJet 540 driver.

An updated DeskJet 540 driver is available for download from the following Microsoft File Transfer Protocol (FTP) site: 

   ftp://ftp.microsoft.com/Services/whql/drivers/win95/printr/
The filename is A882.exe.
