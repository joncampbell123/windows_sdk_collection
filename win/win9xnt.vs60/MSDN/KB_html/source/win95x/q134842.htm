Leonardo da Vinci Screen Saver Displays Tank Image Briefly

 Last reviewed: January 26, 1996
Article ID: Q134842
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows 95
  • Microsoft Plus! For Windows 95

SYMPTOMS

When the Leonardo da Vinci screen saver is running, you may see an image that appears to be out of sequence.

The Leonardo da Vinci screen saver displays a series of manuscript images in a loop. Near the end of the sequence, an image of a manuscript drawing of a tank appears briefly near the center of the screen.

MORE INFORMATION

The last image in the sequence is of a flying wing positioned at the bottom right corner of the screen. The tank image appears briefly near the center of the screen, and then the sequence begins again with the tank manuscript appearing correctly from the left side of the screen.

STATUS

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Microsoft Plus! for Windows 95. We are researching this problem and will post new information here in the Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.
