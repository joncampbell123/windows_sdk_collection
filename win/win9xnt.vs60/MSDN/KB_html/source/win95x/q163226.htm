PaperPort Driver Setup Button May Be Unavailable

 Last reviewed: July 22, 1997
Article ID: Q163226
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows 95

SUMMARY

When you install the PaperPort driver that is included in Microsoft Windows 95 Service Pack 1, you are required to enter your name and the serial number of your PaperPort product. The Continue button is unavailable until this information is entered.

MORE INFORMATION

The PaperPort driver included in the Drivers\Misc\Pportdrv folder on the Windows 95 Service Pack 1 CD-ROM is made exclusively for the Visioneer PaperPort scanner. Please contact Visioneer if you need assistance in finding the serial number for your product.
