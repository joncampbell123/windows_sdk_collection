Channels Reversed on Sound Galaxy Washington 16 Sound Card

 Last reviewed: October 4, 1995
Article ID: Q132605
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows 95

SYMPTOMS

When you play MIDI files on an Aztech Sound Galaxy Washington 16 sound card, the left and right channels may be reversed.

CAUSE

This is a known problem with the Aztech Sound Galaxy Washington 16 sound card.

RESOLUTION

For information about possible resolutions to this problem, please contact the vendor who provided the sound card before you contact Aztech.
