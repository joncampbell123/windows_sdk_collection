Icon Arrangement Not Saved in Dial-Up Networking Folder

 Last reviewed: August 30, 1996
Article ID: Q134565
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows 95

SYMPTOMS

If you arrange the icons in the Dial-Up Networking folder, the next time you open the folder, the icons are rearranged.

CAUSE

The Dial-Up Networking folder automatically arranges its icons when you open the folder.

STATUS

This behavior is by design.

MORE INFORMATION

Although you cannot specify the placement of icons in the Dial-Up Networking folder, you can specify the order in which the connectoid icons are arranged.
