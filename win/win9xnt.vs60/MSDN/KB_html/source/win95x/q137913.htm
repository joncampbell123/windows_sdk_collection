Problems Printing to PCPI Laser Image 1030 Printer

 Last reviewed: October 10, 1995
Article ID: Q137913
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows 95

SYMPTOMS

When you use the Windows PostScript printer driver to print to an older PCPI Laser Image series 1030 printer, the printout may be garbled.

CAUSE

This printer is not fully compatible with Windows 95.

MORE INFORMATION

The printer manufacturer has stated that the driver for this printer will not be updated. For additional information about PCPI products, please contact the manufacturer.
