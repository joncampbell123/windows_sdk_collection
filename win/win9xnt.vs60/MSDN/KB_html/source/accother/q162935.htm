ADT/ODE: Final Help Workshop Training Card Does Not Close

 Last reviewed: October 20, 1997
Article ID: Q162935
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Office 97 Developer Edition Tools
  • Microsoft Access Developer's Toolkit, version 7.0

SYMPTOMS

Moderate: Requires basic macro, coding, and interoperability skills.

When you use the Training Cards on the Help Menu for assistance in creating a project in the Help Workshop, when you get to the last of these cards, the Close button does not dismiss the final Training Card.

RESOLUTION

The only way to dismiss the final Training Card for Creating A Project is to close it manually by clicking the Close button in the upper-right corner of the window or by pressing ALT+F4 on the keyboard.

STATUS

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Microsoft Office Developer Edition 97 and Microsoft Access Developer's Toolkit, version 7.0. We are researching this problem and will post new information here in the Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.

MORE INFORMATION

Steps to Reproduce Problem

  1. Open the Microsoft Help Workshop from ODE Tools (or ADT for version 7.0).

  2. Click Training Cards on the Help Menu, and then select Creating A Project.

  3. Follow the instructions on the Training Cards, until you get to the last one that begins with "Done!..." and contains a Close button.

  4. Click the Close button. Note that the Training Card does not close as expected.
