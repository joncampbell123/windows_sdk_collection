PRB: "File Not Open" Message After Use in RQBE

 Last reviewed: April 17, 1995
Article ID: Q99759
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft FoxPro for MS-DOS, versions 2.0, 2.5, 2.5a, 2.5b

SYMPTOMS

When attempting to open a file in a work area other than the one from which it was previously used during an RQBE session, the error "File not open" is returned.

RESOLUTION

To solve the problem, delete the index.
