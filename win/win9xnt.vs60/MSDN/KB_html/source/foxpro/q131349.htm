FIX: Printing Problems with 300 DPI or More Resolution

 Last reviewed: October 20, 1997
Article ID: Q131349
2.50b 2.50c 2.60a MACINTOSH kbprint kbfixlist kbbuglist

The information in this article applies to:

  • Microsoft FoxPro for Macintosh, versions 2.5b, 2.5c, and 2.6a

SYMPTOMS

When printing a Report Form or an @... SAY bitmap, FoxPro for Macintosh cannot print graphics reliably at resolutions over 75 Dots Per Inch (DPI). PICT graphics look jagged, fuzzy, or skewed.

RESOLUTION

Converting PICT files to .BMP files may yield some improvements in the picture quality if printing to a laser printer. However, results vary from graphic to graphic.

STATUS

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in the Microsoft products listed at the beginning of this article. This problem was corrected in Visual FoxPro 3.0b for Macintosh.
