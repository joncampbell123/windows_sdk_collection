Disk Space Requirements of FoxPro Ver 2.6 for Windows

 Last reviewed: June 26, 1995
Article ID: Q116020
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft FoxPro for Windows, version 2.6

SUMMARY

Listed below are the free disk space requirements in kilobytes (K) for the complete and minimum installations of FoxPro for Windows.

MORE INFORMATION

Complete Installation

NOTE: Running the ALLUNPAK.BAT file, located in the SAMPLES subdirectory, to expand sample files and bitmaps requires 5,864K of disk space in addition to the amount listed below for the FPW26 directory.

Drive containing the FPW26 directory: 19,556K Drive containing the WINDOWS directory: 838K

TOTAL (disk space for FoxPro and Windows files, including files expanded by ALLUNPAK.BAT): 26,258K

Minimum Installation

Drive containing the FPW26 directory: 7,381K Drive containing the WINDOWS directory: 278K

TOTAL (disk space for FoxPro and Windows files): 7,659K

Additional reference words: FoxWin 2.60 available hard hd install setup
