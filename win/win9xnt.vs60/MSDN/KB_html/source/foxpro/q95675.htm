PROAPI16.EXE Not Used in FoxPro 2.5 for MS-DOS

 Last reviewed: April 17, 1995
Article ID: Q95675
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft FoxPro for MS-DOS, version 2.5

On page A-7 in Appendix A of the FoxPro 2.5 for MS-DOS "Developer's Guide", error number 1709, "Cannot load PROAPI16.EXE" is incorrectly documented.

PROAPI16.EXE is no longer used in FoxPro 2.5 for MS-DOS, as a result of a switch from the ERGO MS-DOS extender, used in FoxPro versions 1.0x and 2.0 for MS-DOS, to the PHAR LAP MS-DOS extender.
