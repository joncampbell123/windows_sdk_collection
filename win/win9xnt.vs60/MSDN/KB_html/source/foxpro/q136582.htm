Help Converting from FoxPro 2.x to Visual FoxPro 3.0

 Last reviewed: September 12, 1995
Article ID: Q136582
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Visual FoxPro for Windows, version 3.0

SUMMARY

For information on converting from FoxPro version 2.6 to Visual FoxPro, choose Technical Reference from the Help Contents menu. Then click Upgrading, and click Converting from FoxPro 2.6. In this topic, choose "Converting FoxPro 2.6 Files."

MORE INFORMATION

The "Converting FoxPro 2.6 Files" topic lists five types of file conversions: projects, screens, reports, labels, and foundation READs. Click one of the links, or read all the Conversion topics in order by clicking the right Browse button (>>). Once you've converted the individual files, use the "Redesigning Converted FoxPro 2.6 Files" topic to take advantage of the new Visual FoxPro features.
