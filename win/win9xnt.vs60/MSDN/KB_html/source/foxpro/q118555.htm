FOXR.EXE Is Not Included in Development Version of FoxPro

 Last reviewed: February 28, 1997
Article ID: Q118555
2.50 2.50a 2.50b 2.60 2.60a MS-DOS kbtool kbprg kbdocerr

The information in this article applies to:

  • Microsoft FoxPro for MS-DOS, versions 2.5x, 2.6, 2.6a

Under the topic "FoxPro Loaders" on page 2-3 in the FoxPro for MS-DOS "Installation and Configuration Guide," the documentation incorrectly states that the FOX.EXE and FOXR.EXE loader programs are included with the development version of FoxPro for MS-DOS.

The FOXR.EXE loader program can only be obtained through the purchase of the FoxPro for MS-DOS Distribution Kit.
