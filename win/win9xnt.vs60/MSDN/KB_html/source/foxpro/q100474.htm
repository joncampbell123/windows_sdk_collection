FoxGraph Is No Longer Supported by Microsoft

 Last reviewed: April 17, 1995
Article ID: Q100474
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft FoxPro for MS-DOS, versions 2.0 and 2.5
  • Microsoft FoxGraph for MS-DOS, version 1.0

With the release of FoxPro 2.5 for MS-DOS, Microsoft has discontinued sales and support for FoxGraph. Development of the upgrade for FoxGraph, called "Perspective for FoxPro," will be assumed by 3D Graphics.

For general information or to place an order, call 3D Graphics at (310) 459-7949 or (310) 459-5822 (fax). For technical support, call 3D Graphics at (310) 471-6331 or (310) 471-7531 (fax).
