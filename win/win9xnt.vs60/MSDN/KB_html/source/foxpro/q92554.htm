Software Disk Caches Compatible with FoxPro

 Last reviewed: April 17, 1995
Article ID: Q92554
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft FoxPro for MS-DOS, version 2.0

This article lists the disk caching programs that are compatible with FoxPro. The products are listed in alphabetical order by product name. For more information in improving FoxPro performance with a disk cache, refer to FoxPro online Help and to the FoxPro "Developer's Guide."

Some of the products listed here are manufactured by vendors independent of Microsoft; we make no warranty, implied or otherwise, regarding these products' performance and reliability.
