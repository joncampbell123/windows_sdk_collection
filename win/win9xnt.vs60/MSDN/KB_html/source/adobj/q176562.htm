Article ID: Q176562
The information in this article applies to:
  • ActiveX Data Objects (ADO), version 1.5
  • Remote Data Service for ADO, versions 1.0, 1.1, 1.5

SUMMARY

CLSIDView provides a simple, non-hierarchical view of registered components that allows you to check the properties of the components without having to cross-reference the ProgIds and Clsids in Regedit.exe. In addition to displaying information about your component, you can register, unregister, mark safe, or forcibly remove all related keys for your component. CLSIDView is for use by individuals familiar with building COM components in Visual Basic or Visual C++.
