X400: How to Obtain CCITT X.400 & X.25 Red Books

 Last reviewed: October 20, 1997
Article ID: Q86962
3.00 MS-DOS kb3rdparty

The information in this article applies to:

  • Microsoft Mail Gateway to X.400, version 3.0

All Consultative Committee for International Telephone and Telegraph (CCITT) X.400, X.200, and X.25 1984 recommendations can be obtained from the following source: 

   United Nations Bookstore
   Room GA 32B
   New York, NY 10017
Some training programs also offer the sale of the Red Book recommendations.
