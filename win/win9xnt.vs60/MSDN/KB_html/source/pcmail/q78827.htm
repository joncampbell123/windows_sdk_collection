PC Gen: Two Versions of Hayes 9600 V-Series Modem

 Last reviewed: October 4, 1994
Article ID: Q78827
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Mail for PC Networks, versions 2.1e, 3.0, 3.0b, 3.2, and 3.2a

Hayes Microcomputer Products, Inc. has marketed two versions of the 9600 V-series modem. There are no physical differences between the two versions; they are identical except that the newer version contains additional speed checking capabilities. This additional capability is called Automatic Speed Buffering.

You can determine the difference between the two versions by looking at the model sticker on the bottom of the modem. Earlier versions are marked as 215AA; later versions are marked as 1000US.

For more information about Hayes modems, call Hayes Microcomputer Technical Assistance at (404) 441-1617.

Hayes modems are manufactured by Hayes Microcomputer Products, Inc., a vendor independent of Microsoft; we make no warranty, implied or otherwise, regarding this product's performance or reliability.
