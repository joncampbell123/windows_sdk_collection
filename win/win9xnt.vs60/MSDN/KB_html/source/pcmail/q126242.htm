PC Win: New Mail Interval Does Not Accept White Space

 Last reviewed: October 17, 1995
Article ID: Q126242
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Mail for Windows, versions 3.2

SYMPTOMS

From the Mail menu, if you choose Options, the Check for New Mail option will be displayed. If you select OK, the following message may be displayed even if the number visible in the Check for New Mail option is between 1 and 9999: 

   Stop The new mail interval requires a number between 1 and 9999.

CAUSE

The Check For New Mail entry cannot accept white space.

RESOLUTION

Clear and re-enter only the number, without white space.

Additional reference words: 3.20 whitespace
