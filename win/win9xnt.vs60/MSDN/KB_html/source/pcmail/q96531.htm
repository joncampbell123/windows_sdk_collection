PC WRmt: Using Mail Remote with Schedule+

 Last reviewed: November 1, 1995
Article ID: Q96531
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Mail Remote for Windows, version 3.2

When you use version 3.2 of Microsoft Mail Remote for Windows without being connected to the network, you cannot initiate a meeting request with version 1.0 of Microsoft Schedule+ for Windows.

You can receive and respond to meeting requests, but they will not automatically be booked in the calendar file.

If the Schedule+ Reminders utility is used with multiple mail drivers, the sign-in prompt may be generated twice.
