PC DB: WA0641: Database Maintenance Utilities

 Last reviewed: April 3, 1996
Article ID: Q99121
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Mail for Windows, versions 2.1x, 3.0, and 3.2.

The application note titled "Database Maintenance Utilities," WA0641, describes operational details for utilities used to repair and maintain the database for Microsoft Mail for PC Networks. The procedures described apply to versions 2.1 and later of Microsoft Mail except where noted.

To obtain this application note in the United States, contact Microsoft Product Support Services.
