FAX: Cover Sheet Size Requirements in Inches

 Last reviewed: October 20, 1997
Article ID: Q119310
3.00 MS-DOS kb3rdparty

The information in this article applies to:

  • Microsoft Mail Gateway to FAX, version 3.0

Page 47 of the "Microsoft Mail Gateway to FAX Administrator's Guide," version 3.0, lists the maximum coversheet size as 1712 pels wide by 800 pels high. This equals 17.83 inches wide by 8.33 inches high.
