PROFS: How to Configure Free/Busy Exchange on VMHOST

 Last reviewed: October 20, 1997
Article ID: Q118458
3.40 MS-DOS kb3rdparty

The information in this article applies to:

  • Microsoft Mail Gateway to IBM PROFS and OfficeVision, version 3.4

SUMMARY

Version 3.4 of the PROFS gateway can exchange Free/Busy scheduling information with Schedule+. The host component is the Calendar Service Machine (MS-CSM); this accesses the calendars of the host PROFS users for complete Free/Busy times distribution (all users' times), as well as incremental Free/Busy time distributions (only calendars that have changed over the interval period). To know when to process incremental Free/Busy times distributions, the MS-CSM machine looks at the time settings in the WAKEUP DATA A file.

*                           SAT, SUN
