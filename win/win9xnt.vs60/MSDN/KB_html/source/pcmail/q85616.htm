FAX: Major Limitations of Microsoft Mail Gateway to Fax

 Last reviewed: October 20, 1997
Article ID: Q85616
3.00 MS-DOS kb3rdparty

The information in this article applies to:

  • Microsoft Mail Gateway to FAX, version 3.0

Here is a list of the major concerns customers have expressed about the Microsoft Mail Gateway to Fax:

  1. Inability to handle more than one fax board

  2. Inability to deliver to individual mailboxes

  3. Inability to view faxes from Windows mail client
