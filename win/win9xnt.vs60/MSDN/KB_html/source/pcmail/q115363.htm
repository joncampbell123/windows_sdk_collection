X400: Selecting Customized Mapping Scrolls Admin Window Up

 Last reviewed: October 20, 1997
Article ID: Q115363
3.20 MS-DOS kb3rdparty

The information in this article applies to:

  • Microsoft Mail Gateway to X.400, version 3.2

After starting X400ADM.EXE, when you select Customized Mapping (with the mouse or Alt-C) the text screen (from the bottom of the X.400 Gateway Administrator window and up) scrolls up, resulting in the window's title bar scrolling off the screen leaving a black line that represents the bottom of the original window.

This occurs only when you open the Customized Mapping window for the first time, and appears to be noticeable only on 386 or slower machines. Although distracting, it introduces no errors or problems. The screen is repainted correctly.
