OL97: Duplicate Meetings in Calendar

 Last reviewed: January 19, 1998
Article ID: Q166741
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Outlook 97

SYMPTOMS

When you create a new meeting and invite at least one other person, two entries for the same meeting may appear in your Calendar.

CAUSE

The option to save copies of replies with the original message is selected. As a result, the meeting request that you sent is saved in the Calendar and appears on the same day with the original.

RESOLUTION

To prevent this from happening follow these steps:

  1. On the Tools menu, click Options.

  2. Click the Sending tab.

  3. Click "In folders other than the Inbox, save replies with original message" to deselect it, and then click OK.

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Microsoft Outlook 97.

REFERENCES

For more information about scheduling meetings, type "meetings" in The Office Assistant, click Search, and then click "Schedule an appointment, meeting, or event" to view the topic.
