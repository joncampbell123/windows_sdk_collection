OL97: Anniversary or Birthday not Held in Timex Data Link Wizard

 Last reviewed: January 19, 1998
Article ID: Q168951
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Outlook 97

SUMMARY

In Microsoft Outlook 97, the information from your last export to the Timex Data Link Watch is held in the Import/Export Wizard for your next export. However, if you have a contact with both the Anniversary and Birthday fields filled in, only one will be selected the next time you export.

MORE INFORMATION

After you export data the first time, information you selected will be available the next time you export to the Data Link Watch. That is, all previously exported items are selected or marked for export.

When a contact has both an Anniversary and a Birthday, only one of these items will be selected for export.

To ensure all of your information is exported, you must step through the Wizard and select the Anniversary or Birthday that is not marked for export.

STATUS

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in the products listed at the beginning of this article. We are researching this problem and will post new information here in the Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.
