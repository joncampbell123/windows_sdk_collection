HP LaserJet 4 PCL Driver Does Not Have a Lower Tray Option

 Last reviewed: May 9, 1997
Article ID: Q104514
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows NT operating system version 3.1
  • Microsoft Windows NT Advanced Server version 3.1

The Windows NT Hewlett-Packard (HP) LaserJet 4 PCL driver does not have a selection for Lower Tray.

The tray selections available with the HP LaserJet 4 PCL driver are the following: 

   Upper Paper Tray
   Auto
   Manual Paper Feed
   Envelope Manual Feed
   Envelope Feeder
To use the optional lower tray that may come with a HP LaserJet 4M printer, the LaserJet 4Si PCL driver must be used. The Windows NT HP LaserJet 4 PostScript driver does have an option for Lower Tray.
