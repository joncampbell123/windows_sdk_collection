Quicken: GP Fault in QW.EXE During Transaction

 Last reviewed: May 15, 1997
Article ID: Q114727

The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows NT version 3.1

SYMPTOMS

While entering a transaction in version 3.0 of Quicken for Windows under Windows NT, a general protection (GP) fault in QW.EXE appears.

CAUSE

The Quickfill feature is enabled. Quickfill is used to complete the entry of a transaction as soon as you type enough characters for it to be identified.

WORKAROUND

Disable Quickfill by doing the following:

  1. From the Edit menu choose Preferences.
2. Choose the Quickfill icon. 3. Choose Enable/Disable Quickfill options.
