Word Wrap Incorrect When Printing from Internet Explorer

 Last reviewed: October 15, 1997
Article ID: Q156424
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Internet Explorer version 2.0
*\ Bug 26518

SYMPTOMS

HTML pages printed from Internet Explorer 2.0 for Windows NT may fail to word wrap correctly when the same pages are printed consecutively. The first print job will print correctly, but the text may not line wrap correctly when printing additional copies of the same page. The problem is most noticeable when a paragraph of text is located immediately under a link.

RESOLUTION

To workaround this problem, use the refresh button after submitting each print job or upgrade to Internet Explorer 3.0

STATUS

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in Internet Explorer version 2.0. We are researching this problem and will post new information here in the Microsoft Knowledge Base as it becomes available.
