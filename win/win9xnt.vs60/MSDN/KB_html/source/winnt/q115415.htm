Maynstream 1350Q Tape Drive Fails to Backup or Restore

 Last reviewed: May 15, 1997
Article ID: Q115415
The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows NT version 3.1

SYMPTOMS

Your Maynard Maynstream 1350Q tape drive fails to backup and restore successfully under Windows NT version 3.1. The tape drive continuously rewinds. The drive status is constantly busy.

CAUSE

Your Maynard tape drive has a firmware revision prior to version 6.0 (28077- 003). Although your tape drive appears on the Windows NT 3.1 hardware compatibility list (HCL), only version 6.0 of the firmware was tested.

RESOLUTION

Obtain a newer revision of the firmware for your tape drive.
