Microsoft Mail (16 or 32 Bit) Cannot Process Messages

 Last reviewed: May 7, 1997
Article ID: Q101627

The information in this article applies to:
  • Microsoft Windows NT operating system, version 3.1
  • Microsoft Windows NT Advanced Server, version 3.1
  • Microsoft Mail

SYMPTOMS

An attempt to open, save, or delete a message in Microsoft Mail fails. Mail displays the "broken network" icon in the lower right corner of the window and Mail does not download new messages to your computer.

CAUSE

There is insufficient free disk space on the drive that stores the Mail message file (*.MMF).

RESOLUTION

Delete files to increase the amount of free disk space on the drive that stores the MMF.
