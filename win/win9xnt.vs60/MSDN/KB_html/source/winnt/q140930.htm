Chinese Characters Rotated 90 Degrees in Word 97

 Last reviewed: September 5, 1997
Article ID: Q140930
The information in this article applies to:
  • Pan Chinese Windows NT Workstation version 4.0
  • Microsoft Word 97 for Windows

SYMPTOMS

Chinese characters typed in Microsoft Word 97 rotate 90 degrees.

RESOLUTION

To resolve this problem, select the Chinese characters and then change the font to the Chinese Font set.
