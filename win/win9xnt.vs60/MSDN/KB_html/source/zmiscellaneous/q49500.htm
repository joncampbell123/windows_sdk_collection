List of Anti-Virus Software Vendors

 Last reviewed: February 18, 1998
Article ID: Q49500

SUMMARY

Anti-virus software is software specifically designed for the detection and prevention of virus programs. Because new virus programs are created all the time, many makers of anti-virus products offer periodic updates of their software to their customers. Microsoft strongly recommends that you use anti-virus software on your computer.

This article lists a few of the suppliers of anti-virus software.

Products that detect macro viruses are noted. For information on Word macro viruses, please see the following article in the Microsoft Knowledge Base: 

   ARTICLE-ID: Q134727
   TITLE     : What to Do If You Have a Macro Virus
