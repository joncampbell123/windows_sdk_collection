Lobbies and Environments

ImagiNation currently supports two gameplaying environments which will act as DirectPlay lobbies, CyberPark and GameXpress (code name). Both of these environments encourage and support online social activities and the development of communities of interest. As you can probably tell, we are big cheerleaders for social interaction centered around gameplay. Within both of these environments, it is not uncommon for fan clubs, bulletin boards, or special-interest groups to form around specific game content - which all adds up to a very loyal following of your online title!

CyberPark is ImagiNation's rich, 3-D Internet world that offers dozens of multiplayer games, real-world entertainment and community-building experiences (here we go with the socialization element again). In the park, visitors can create full-body avatars, selecting hairstyles and accessories to personalize their online presence.

All of the games available for play in CyberPark are multiplayer. Players find opponents and team members through sponsored tournaments, special events and contests, or even by just walking around in the park and chatting.

CyberPark's open and inviting virtual environment allows players to navigate through diverse neighborhoods and participate in activities within a themed area. The park is filled with online hosts and game coaches that provide around-the-clock support for visitors, directing them to activities such as your game. CyberPark will be accessible later this summer through the combination of a CD-ROM and a connection to AOL or other ISP.
GameXpress
GameXpress is a code name for ImagiNation's rapid-access interactive entertainment platform. 		It is designed so that players can quickly download and connect to play games with others, and enables them to have immediate access to a handful of ImagiNation's most popular multiplayer game titles. GameXpress members can also participate in chat rooms, converse during gameplay, as well as simply "watch and learn" while others play.
