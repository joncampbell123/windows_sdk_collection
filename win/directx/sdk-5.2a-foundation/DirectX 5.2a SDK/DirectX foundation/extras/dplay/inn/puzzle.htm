Spunky's Shuffle

In this energetic puzzle game, players are pitted against each other in a race to align rows of colorful tiles. With attack and defense strategies, varied game play, secret moves, a range of possible tasks and a stereo musical score that enhances the excitement, Spunky's Shuffle is a challenging and addictive game for all ages.
