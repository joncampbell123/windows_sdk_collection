Strategy Games


 DOME-HEADS OF THE WORLD, UNITE!

Online is a title exclusive to ImagiNation! that is directly derived from the original award-winning box version. ImagiNation's online version will have exclusive graphics, custom inventions and team play.

This god-like game allows players to control groups of follically-challenged beings, the Baldies, and compete against the Hairies.

Players do this by building houses, laboratories and barracks, breeding Baldies and creating inventions to attack their opponents until the Hairies are plucked from the land.

There will be additional features available with the online version, including contests, tournaments and team play, making this a wacky and unique gaming experience.


MissionForce: CyberStorm(TM)

 Cyberstorm players create and control (giant robots) and Bioderms (artificially created human-like pilots) and send them on missions throughout the galaxy. Players accept missions ranging from mining ore to increase wealth, to search and destroy of Cybrids (non-human controlled robots) for salvage.


Trophy Bass 2 (TM)

Fishing enthusiasts and landlubbers alike can try to outsmart 'the big one' in the multiplayer version of Trophy Bass. A true fishing simulation, Trophy Bass 2 features 10 different lakes and hundreds of lure, rod and line combinations.

Players can scout the overhead lake map for likely fishing holes and fine-tune their choice spots using the water temperature and sonar fish finder.
The perfect Place to find a Trophy Bass competitor is at the Game Lodge.

Thrash


Thrash, under development for ImagiNation by Vipah Interactive, is an action-maze game. Players fly hovercraft, negotiate mazes and take on other players in a fast-paced, real-time game.





While flying, players locate weapons, special shields and jump gates that move them to other spots in the maze. Players learn to navigate the maze to get the best supplies and prevent their enemies from getting those same supplies. Players can also form alliances, working competitively or cooperatively.
