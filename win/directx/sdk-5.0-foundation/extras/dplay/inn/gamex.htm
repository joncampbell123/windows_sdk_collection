GameXpress

In the lobbies of GameXpress, visitors select a room and choose the level of game they wish to play. Players can also change their online profile and search for other members.
In each game room, players can spend their time playing, chatting or simply checking each other out. The chat feature also lets you send sounds to the other people in the room. If you're not sure you want to play, you can choose to watch or join a game.
