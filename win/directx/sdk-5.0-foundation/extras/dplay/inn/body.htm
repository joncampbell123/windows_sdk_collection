What is ImagiNation About?

For developers, our primary goal is to provide and nurture dynamic online environments to suport great content. Next, we want to make it quick and easy (and lots of fun!) for game developers to develop content for mass distribution by AOL and other online and Internet Service Providers.
ImagiNation/AOL is committed to providing the most exciting, compelling and comprehensive collection of online games, entertainment, and communities worldwide.
By working directly with Microsoft to integrate our proprietary SDK extensions with the popular DirectPlay platform, we are hoping to make life much easier for you, the developer. If you are already familiar with the Microsoft standards, you will soon be able to take advantage of our (ImagiNation and AOL) value-added online games features; such as tournament scoring, and virtual currency interfaces, without having to develop differently for different platforms. Talk about a headache saver!
ImagiNation and DirectPlay

 Simply stated, we want to standardize the game development process as much as you do, which is why we are supporting DirectPlay, and sprinkling our technical expertise into the mix.
