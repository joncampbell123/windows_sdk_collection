Light House Demo

Wind Conditions:

LightHouse is a good example of 2D/3D/sound integration. The boat, bird, and anemometer are 3D objects rendered and coordinated with the 2D elements of the scene. You may click on the bird and drag it around. Three small seed sound files are attributed and mixed for the background ambient sound. A VB slider influences all aspects of the animation by representing the wind intensity.
